Before you reach out

COMMON
QUESTIONS

How quickly can you start my project?

Usually within 1–2 weeks of signing. We take on a limited number of projects at a time to ensure quality and focus. Get in touch now to reserve your spot.

What do you need from me to get started?

Just a brief description of what you want to build, your timeline, and a rough budget range. We'll take it from there — we've helped clients who came with nothing more than a napkin sketch.

How does payment work?

We typically work on a 50% deposit to begin, 50% on delivery structure for smaller projects. Larger projects may be split across milestones. We accept bank transfer or PayPal.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely. We've worked with clients across the US and remotely. All project communication happens async via email, Slack, or your preferred tool — timezone differences are no problem.

What if I'm not sure what I need?

That's what the discovery call is for. We'll talk through your business goals, and I'll recommend the right approach. No pressure, no commitment — just a conversation.
