The founder

WAI
YAN MIN

I'm Wai Yan Min — a Burmese-American full-stack developer and Computer Science student at Mt. San Antonio College. Growing up between two cultures shaped how I approach design and communication: I bring precision, speed, and genuine care for the communities I build for.

Today I serve as CTO at MYTTA Foundation, where I architected and maintain their entire digital platform — the public website, internal tools, and all technical decisions. That experience showed me exactly what businesses need and what they rarely get from agencies.

I founded Tectonic with a small, focused team of 2–3 builders to change that. Affordable pricing, 1–2 week delivery, modern designs, and direct communication — you always talk to the developer, not a middleman.
