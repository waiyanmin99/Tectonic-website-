Common questions

FAQ

How long does a typical project take?

Most website projects take 1–2 weeks from kickoff to launch. Web apps and mobile apps typically take 3–8 weeks depending on complexity. We'll give you a specific timeline after our discovery call.

What's the process for getting started?

Simple — fill out the contact form or email me directly. We'll schedule a short discovery call, I'll send over a proposal and timeline, and once you approve we get to work.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes. All projects include post-launch support (30 or 60 days depending on the plan). Beyond that, we offer monthly maintenance retainers for content updates, security patches, and new features.

Can I make changes during the project?

Minor adjustments are part of the process and expected. Major scope changes may affect timeline and budget — we'll always communicate clearly before any changes take effect.

Do I own everything after the project is done?

100%. Once the project is paid in full, you own all code, designs, and assets outright. No licensing fees, no lock-in.
